Who We Are?

We are a team of passionate and dedicated students pursuing our Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) from Graphic Era Hill University. As part of our Project-Based Learning (PBL) initiative, we collaborated to create an innovative e-commerce website that specializes in selling a wide range of clothing for all age groups and styles. Our goal was not just to build a project for academic credit, but to develop a real-world solution that reflects current market demands and applies what we’ve learned in a practical way. From designing the user interface to managing backend logic, we have worked diligently to ensure our platform is user-friendly, functional, and responsive. Our team consists of: Sagar Rana (Team Leader) – BCA Section C Kunal Chand (Team Member) – BCA Section B Rohit Kumar Shah (Team Member) – BCA Section C Together, we explored modern web development tools, learned the importance of teamwork, and tackled real-time challenges while building this platform. We are proud of our accomplishment and excited about the possibilities this project holds for future enhancement and learning.

We are committed to staying up-to-date with the latest fashion trends and providing exceptional customer service to ensure our customers look and feel their best.
